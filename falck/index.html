Background

Falck sells home security systems to Danish households. Competition on this market is tough and demands that new sales leads are continually generated. Typically through short term (sales) campaigns. This time Falck wanted to develop a longer lasting and differentiating one – going live January 2014 and re-vitalized with marketing bursts during the year.

Target Group

The target group is primarily house-owners all over Denmark. Some alarms are sold directly online, but typically a sale is based on a Falck Alarm consultant visiting the lead in their home suggesting a solution and after that the final contract is agreed upon and the alarm installed.

The Insight

There are many home security system operators in Denmark. Communication was typically rational, featuring the technical aspects of the alarm system – and the price. To differentiate we needed to lift Falck above this and bring the relevance of an alarm solution closer to home. Literally. Enabling the consumer to judge for him-/herself the reason to have cause for alarm – or not.

The Solution

To provide a data-enriched personal and individual digital experience, which would generate qualified leads for telemarketing follow-up, booking of meetings and eventually sales of a Falck home security system.

The Concept

Using all the big data available for a specific address, we created an exciting and engaging interactive digital platform. Asking: ”Check if you have cause for alarm in regard to the risk of burglary at your address”. And giving the answer based on credible data.

The Data

The digital solution use real time data and calculations from a variety of public sources such as: Google street view, data on the individual house/apartment from Bygnings- og Boligregistret, crime statistics from Danmarks Statistik, Rigspolitiets statistiksystem, Eurostat, as well as consumer profiles fra GEOMATIC’s conzoom classifications.
